WHAT IS TARS?

TARS is an autonomous unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV) I developed over two years for a competition called TEKNOFEST HIGH SCHOOL UAV COMPETITON.

Throughout the years, TARS's design, mission, and team changed, but its shape always was chosen as QuadCopter.

At the latest version, TARS was assigned to carry, and drop three tennis balls autonomously to the separeted areas. For this mission, a vertical mission mechanism designed. After presentations to jury, we got production support, then we build it even though all challanges and made it ready for rising to sky.

Technical Informations

Type: QuadCopter

Project Duration: 2 Years

Active Years: 2020-2022

Weight: 1189g

Payload: 3 Tennis Ball

Control Options: Manuel, Half and Full Autonomous
